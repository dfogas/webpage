On Style

Sense Of Style: Steven Pinker talk - whatever you write, do it in style! Seriously if you write anything for humans, bear points of this talk in mind.

  1. Classical style of story telling - you open window to observer's mind for reader
  2. String of words vs. Web of Thoughts - how do we tackle this? by hyperlinks?
  3. Curse of knowledge - by knowing what monad is, you magically lose your ability to explain it to anybody else - kudos to Crockford
  4. Tacit, evolving conventions rather than formal system d-oh
